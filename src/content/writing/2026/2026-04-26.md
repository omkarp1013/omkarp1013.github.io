---
title: Systems
---
Recently I have begun to think of everything as a system. After all, we are surrounded by and constantly interacting with systems: biological systems (other humans, animals, plants), computer systems (smartphones, computers, AI), economic systems (capitalism, socialism), and many more. As such, if you are trying to understanding something or someone, it is probably worth modeling this as a system and trying to understand its design, bottlenecks, incentives, mesaures of revenue and profitability, biases, shortcomings, etc.
