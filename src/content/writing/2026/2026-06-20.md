---
title: You  
---
You are the only person that can truly change the course of your life (no matter how small or big this change actually is). Obviously, I am not talking about random events that my impact your life, but moreseo along the lines of making a change in habits, making a change in one of your relationship(s), etc.

If you want to change something about yourself, the last barrier to doing so will be you. No matter how much you are pushed by someone, someone does something for you, or more generally if there is an external factor that is helping you achieve what you want, there is still no greater force than the voice inside your head telling you what to do. Use it with agency.
