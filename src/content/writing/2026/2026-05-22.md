---
title: Stimulation
---
Nowadays, it is quite easy to be constantly stimulated. We are surrounded by media in various forms on our cellphones, computers, TVs, etc. If you are running, you can listen to music or a podcast. If you are waiting for something (e.g. code) to finish running, you can scroll on your phone, read a newsletter, watch a quick YouTube video, play a quick game, and so on and so forth. Recently, I've been trying to reduce this constant simulation in my life and try as hard as possible to be focused on a single topic at any given time. Multitasking has been proven to negatively affect attention span and stress levels, as well as worsen mental fatigue. I believe your brain is one of the most unique and powerful things in the world, and there is beauty in avoiding the constant stimulation that we are inevitably surrounded by.
