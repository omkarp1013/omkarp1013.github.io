---
title: Imitation Learning 
---
Sometimes, I have the best learning experiences when observing how others do something, mimicking their workflow, and adding in my own twists. This goes hand in hand with surrounding yourself with people that you will learn from (in your personal life, the workplace, etc.). Obviously, you should not blindly copy someone else's process for doing something simply because you like them, or they are successful in another part of of their life, etc. but this can be an effective way to learn from others who have achieved something similar to what you want to achieve.
