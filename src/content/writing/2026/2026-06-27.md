title: Interests
---
I feel like I have relatively diverse (academic) interests. I can spend one hour reading about the economics of hyperscalers in the AI race and the next reading about Alan Greenspan and his tenure at the Federal Reserve. Ultimately, I try to remind myself that my time is finite, and it is probably better (at least for my career versus conversations over dinner) to focus deeply on a few, maybe closely related fields. This way, I can maybe get paid for my knowledge (whether from a job or something else), as well as feel like I can make a concrete contribution to a field. With information being more democratized now than ever, this increasingly feels easier.
