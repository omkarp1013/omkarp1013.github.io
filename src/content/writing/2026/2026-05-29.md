---
title: Decisions
---
It's interesting how a large part of your life is governed by the individual decisions you make. As (a light) example, say you decide to binge-watch a show at midnight instead of going to sleep and end up sleeping at 3am, when you have to wake up at 7am the next morning. This might cause your next day to be unproductive, which in turn might cause you to have to catch up on the day after. Decisions cascade, which is why it is important to try to make every decision as logically as possible. This is obviously much more easily said than done, but this thought exercise (thinking about the downstream effects of a decision) might help.
