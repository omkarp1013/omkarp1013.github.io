---
title: Do It Now
---
If you have something that you want to start (a new workout routine, personal project, travel plans, etc.), you should start making progress towards it as soon as possible. Ideally, you should start *now*. You shouldn't meticulously plan on how you will do said endeavor, or make a detailed schedule. Instead, you should get your hands dirty and actually *do* what you want to do. Your time will pass anyways, and it is likely that you will regret not starting whatever you wanted to start sooner. So do it now.
