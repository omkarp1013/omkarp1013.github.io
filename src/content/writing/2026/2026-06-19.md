---
title: Control 
---
Your attitude is one of the only things in your life that you can control. It is also a formative aspect in terms of how you view the world, how you approach problems, and how you develop and manage relationships. There are several aspects of your life that will inevitably influence your attitude and therefore the factors I just mentioned. The way in which you process these factors using your existing attitude (such as biases, people, day-to-day events, etc.) is extremely important to your future attitude. This iterative process can help improve these aspects of your life and your approach to solving probelms, relationships, etc.
