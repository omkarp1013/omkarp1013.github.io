title: Chapters
---
It is interesting how people sometimes assign chapters to their lives, whether it be the different phases of school (elementary, middle, high, etc.), college, then working, etc. or something similar. In a sense, every hour, every second, every atomic unit of time is a "new beginning" - you are never going to live the past again and every unit of time that you experience in the future is unique and new. So there is no need to get caught up in "chapters" of lives and decide to say, make a drastic change in your life only when a new chapter begins. If you want to do something, [you can just do things](https://omkarp1013.github.io/writing/2026/2026-05-02).
