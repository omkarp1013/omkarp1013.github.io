---
title: Information Democratization
---
It amazes me how much the Internet and now AI have democratized the flow of information. There are fewer and fewer levels of abstraction to access books, interviews, podcasts, and other forms of media that are wonderful sources of learning. I have probably mentioned this before, but a piece of media by (or including) someone with say, 20 years of experience in a field (or just life experience!) effectively distills this information into a few hours. So the fact that billions of people can now access this knowledge continues to amaze me every day. A more short-form example is Twitter, which has made this flow of information continuous, with academia and industry leaders, policymakers, central bankers, writers, poets, artists, etc. giving their thoughts on something new almost *daily*. We are truly in a golden age for information.
